Dog Breeds
beagle A rather cute breed of dog who loves the attention of people and other dogs. As trackers, they like to chase things around like toys or people.
golden retriever A very famous breed of dog due to it being frequently used to help the blind. They are energetic and require alot of physical activity.
poodle This is a small breed of dog but is said to be one of the wisest. They almost do not shed and are very athletic.
